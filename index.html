CHAPLAIN

St. Joseph’s Senior Secondary School Naggalama is a CATHOLIC FOUNDED SCHOOL and therefore follows Catholic Disciplines upon which Academic excellence is built. Our Facilities

Making our students feel at home.

World Class Library

The school has a well-stocked library to enable students and staff carryout research. Our collection of books are relevant to the academic needs of the learners. The renewal of stock of books is done annually and whenever need arises. The school has core course text books for every subjects at both O and A levels.

Well Stocked Clinic

World Health Organisation defines health as the physical, social and mental well-being of an individual and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity. It is impeccably known that the school medical team in co-operation with the school administration strives to keep the students of St. Joseph’s SSS Naggalama, physically, mentally and socially healthy which explains their astounding performance in various curricular and co – curricular activities. We also utilise the services of St. Francis Naggalama Hospital for referral cases.

Comfortable classrooms

The classes are shifting from being teacher-centered to student-centered. The role of the teacher is changing from being a ‘sage on the stage’ to the ‘guide on the side’. Students sit in groups of four. The chairs swivel so that students may more easily look each other in the eye while speaking. Individual white boards help introverted students participate in class and also serve as barriers during assessment time. So far, the results have been good.
